# Privacy Policy for Model2Art

*Last updated: July 2026*

## Overview

Model2Art ("the app") is a mobile application that helps miniature and model painting enthusiasts discover tutorial videos. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

---

## Data We Collect

When you create an account or user profile within the app, we may collect the following personal information:

- First name
- Surname
- Email address

This information is used solely to provide and personalise your in-app experience. We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

If you use the app without creating an account, no personal information is collected.

---

## How We Use Your Data

Personal information collected is used to:

- Create and manage your user profile
- Personalise your content recommendations
- Communicate important updates about the app

---

## Data Storage and Security

Any personal data collected is stored securely. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss or misuse.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights contact us at the email address below.

---

## Third Party Services

The app uses the following third party services which have their own privacy policies:

- **YouTube Data API v3** (Google LLC) — used to fetch video content. [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** (Google LLC) — used to display advertisements. [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## Advertising

The app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings under **Privacy → Apple Advertising**.

Users who choose the limited experience during onboarding will only be shown non-personalised advertisements.

---

## Affiliate Links

The app contains affiliate links to third party retailers including Element Games, Wayland Games and Amazon. Tapping these links opens an external browser. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

---

## Consent

On first launch the app presents a consent screen explaining how your data is used. You may choose:

- **Full consent** — personalised ads and access to all features including future profile functionality
- **Limited experience** — non-personalised ads only, no profile features

You can request a change to your consent preferences at any time by contacting us.

---

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information please contact us so we can remove it.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes where possible. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
